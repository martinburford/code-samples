# Grid

**Important: the `<Grid>` component works on the basis of a 12x wide grid.**

The `<Grid>` component works in a slightly different way to all other components, in as much it as has 2 exports, `<Col>` and `<Row>`. Whilst a `<Row>` acts merely as a containing element, each `<Col>` can receive its own props (as below):
